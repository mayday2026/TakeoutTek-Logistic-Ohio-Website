We are a group of creative engineers who have built a product to change the world.

Food delivery is a very special and important link in the food delivery industry, whether it is for restaurants, customers, or food delivery platforms. Often, there is no safe place to store deliveries, resulting in loss of takeout, delayed delivery, poor thermal insulation, or even theft of food, requiring customers to spend more effort in resolution and an inferior user experience.
‍
A food storage locker is designed for high-traffic environments with multiple people utilizing food delivery services. This product is best suited for urban areas or college campuses with attributes such as little space and high residential density.
